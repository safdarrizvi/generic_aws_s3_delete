Life
Wahi was born in Khujwa Village in Siwan District (Bihar), India. His father was Syed Zahoor Hussain, who served as the regional zamindar and also worked as police inspector for several years. His father also worked at the Iranian Embassy after leaving the police department, and died in January 1962.

Wahi began writing while in middle school. During his time at Patna College, his poetry began to flourish. At first his poetic style was serious, but by 1950, he began to explore more sarcasm and humor. Thereafter he continued the sarcasm and humor genres.

His major poetic influences include Josh Malihabadi andMir Taqi Mir.

Guidance ( Poetry)
In the beginning he taken the guidelines of Late Mirza Anwar. He nurture the initial style of writing poetry in him.
